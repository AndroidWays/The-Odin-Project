Strawberry Shortcake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups strawberries, sliced
  • Whipped cream for topping

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the softened butter and mix until crumbly.
  4. Add the milk and vanilla extract, and stir until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Let it cool, then slice in half horizontally.
  7. Add sliced strawberries and whipped cream between the layers and on top.

Enjoy your strawberry shortcake!
